Facial Detection Using Haar Cascade Algorithm

programmer X Designer

Overview

The project focused on facial feature recognition techniques and evaluating them in terms of accuracy and effectiveness. The goal is to explore the mechanics behind facial recognition.

Tools: Processing, OpenCV Library

Team members:

  • Praneet Atwal – Programmer, facial detection research and exploration.
  • Mahsa Alavi – Research and concept exploration.

Details:

Generally, face recognitions are accurate these days but when it comes to situations such as low image resolution, low contrast image and pose variations the system might fail to
recognize face features. We looked at 3 different research studies for facial recognition and features that are more dominant in the face to allow for accurate facial detection.

Process and Research

We experimented with different images and recorded the results based on the studies. Facial detection works best when detecting eyes as one of the targets. It works least with
detecting the nose. Additionally, having a low-resolution image, angled face, skin color also affects the accuracy at which an algorithm will work to detect the face.
